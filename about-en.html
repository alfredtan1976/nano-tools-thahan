A digital tool for Thai conscripts and families

What This Site Does

Thai Military Countdown helps Thai conscripts, partners, and families estimate discharge dates, track remaining time, and understand common conscription-related documents and timelines.

Our Goal

The goal is to make Thai conscription timelines easier to understand through a mobile-first calculator and practical articles.

Important Note

This is not an official government website. Users should verify official matters with the recruiting office, military unit, or current government notices.

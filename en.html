Quick guide for Thai conscripts and families

What affects a Thai military discharge date?

The main factors are military branch, entry batch, entry type, education documents, Ror Dor training, and any special case such as absence from draft selection.

When do Thai conscription batches start?

The Army and Air Force usually use Batch 1 on May 1 and Batch 2 on November 1. The Navy can use four batches: May 1, August 1, November 1, and February 1.
